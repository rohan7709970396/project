Biography Categories

  • Scholarly chronicles

    In scholarly chronicles, the emphasis is on rigorous research, accuracy, and the use of credible sources. They are typically written by experts or scholars in the field who possess in-depth knowledge and expertise on the subject matter.

  • Intellectual biography

    In an intellectual biography, the author examines the subject's intellectual influences, educational background, and the significant ideas or concepts that shaped their thinking.

  • Historical

    In a historical biography, the author conducts extensive research to gather information from primary and secondary sources, such as letters, diaries, official records, and historical accounts.

  • Narrative biography

    In a narrative biography, the author delves into the subject's personal experiences, relationships, and significant events in their life, crafting a cohesive and captivating story.
